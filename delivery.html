Our Delivery Process

Delivery Methods

We offer two primary delivery methods:

  • Open Trailer Transport: Cost-effective for standard vehicles
  • Enclosed Transport: Secure option for luxury and classic cars

Delivery Timeline

Delivery times vary based on destination:

  • Abuja to Lagos: 2-3 days
  • Abuja to Port Harcourt: 3-4 days
  • Abuja to Kano: 2-3 days
  • National Coverage: 3-7 days depending on location

Safety & Security

We prioritize the safety of your vehicle during transport:

  • All drivers are professionally trained and licensed
  • Vehicles are fully insured during transport
  • GPS tracking available for premium shipments
  • Pre and post-delivery inspection included
  • Secure loading and unloading procedures

Pricing

Our delivery rates are competitive and transparent:

  • Standard Vehicles: ₦ 80,000 - ₦ 150,000 depending on distance
  • Luxury Vehicles: ₦ 120,000 - ₦ 250,000 (includes enclosed transport)
  • Special Requirements: Additional fees may apply
  • Insurance: Included in all deliveries
